Software developer at O-Pit Blast (Feb/22 - Mar/23)

After my internship I was hired by the company. At O-Pit Blast I worked using C# as the main language and WPF to create the front end elements, for this we use the Syncfusion library, we also use agile metodologies to manage the development. I worked on the development of a 3D rock blasting simulation software that helps mining engineers to plan their projects.

Software developer intern at O-Pit Blast (Nov/21 - Feb/22)

O-Pit Blast is a Portuguese software development company for mining engineering which is based in the city of Porto. The company was bought by Forcit and Enaex, which is a Finnish and a Chilean multinational companies in the sector, respectively. In my internship period I was responsible for the development of unit tests for the mathematical functions of our nuget package and their implementation.

Production manager at Paktuall Fardamentos (Jul/18 - Sep/21)

Paktuall is a uniform company that has been in business since 1989. During my time there I was responsible for implementing new methodologies to improve the efficiency and productivity of the team. During this period I learned to work as a team towards a common goal. I also worked with the graphic part of the production, making the artwork that goes to the silkscreen.
